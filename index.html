What's considered an anagram?

  • A string contains at least one character, which can be an upper or lower case letter, number, or symbol
  • Spaces do not count as characters (i.e. 'abc' is an anagram of 'b ac')
  • Uppercase and lowercase letters are considered to be the same letter (i.e. 'ABC' is an anagram of 'cba')
